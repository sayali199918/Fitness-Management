        About

        TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT)

        TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) is a multi-level assessment to assess the following competencies and skills:

        • 1. Competence on the core cognitive processes required for entry-level jobs
        • 2. Industry-specific knowledge and insights
        • 3. Specialisation on skills required to perform various job roles

        NQT Score*

        Performance measure of cognitive skills in the areas of verbal, numerical and reasoning abilities

        Attitudinal Alignment NQT

        Psychometric Test to assess personality traits and dispositions relevant to the workplace

        Industry NQT Score

        Performance measure of the understanding of industry-specific dimensions

        Subject NQT Score

        Performance measure of role-specific skills on the dimensions of knowledge and ability.

          About the Test

        • Candidates with NQT Score will be eligible to apply on the TCS iON Job Listing portal (coming shortly)
        • Mode of Test: In-Centre (TCS iON Authorised Exam Centres)
        • Two-year validity of NQT Score
        • Apply for any entry-level role in any corporate with the NQT Score

          Who can Apply

        • UG, PG and Diploma students in their pre-final and final year
        • Students with any degree and specialisation/discipline
        • Freshers with less than 2 years of experience
        *NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is a prerequisite for all the tests.

        Subject NQT - IT Programming

        Test Coverage:

        Assesses the knowledge and application of programming concepts.

        Programming Logic: Pseudocode, Algorithms, Programming constructs, basic SDLC, implementation using C, C++ and Java

        Hands-on Coding Exercise: In any one of the programming languages like C, C++, Java, Python and PERL

        Who can apply:

        Anyone aspiring to build a career in IT industry as a developer, having a score in this, shows that you have hands-on experience in programming and can learn any language as needed for the job. Most of the IT companies consider this as a mandatory skill to possess.

        Job Opportunities:

        Developer role in IT Industry

        Across any Industry or Job role

        FAQs

        1. What is TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT)? +
        • TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) is an Ability Test that assesses a candidate's competencies and skills. Each candidate who completes the TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) gets an NQT Score. The NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is applicable for all industries and job roles. The TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) is planned to be conducted every quarter. Candidates can take the tests again to improve their previous scores, if desired. There are four Variants - NQT, Attitudinal Alignment NQT (Psychometric Test), Industry NQT and Subject NQT.
          • Note:
            • NQT is the mandatory test to be taken to register for Industry NQT and Subject NQT.
            • NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is a prerequisite to appear for Industry NQTs and Subject NQTs.
            • All tests will be conducted in English language only and there will be no negative marking.
            • Candidates who had appeared for NQT in October 2020 can reappear in February 2021 and in all the upcoming tests.
            • Candidates who have an NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) may choose any test from the Industry NQTs and Subject NQTs.
            • Candidates will be able to view/edit their details and add test(s) pertaining to Industry NQT and/or Subject NQT on/before the registration closure date.
            • Candidates who select higher level skills will not be allowed to select its basic variant. For example, if the candidate chooses Subject NQT - IT Programming then he/she will not be able to choose Subject NQT - Basic Computer Skills.
        2. What is NQT? +
        • NQT is a General Ability Test that assesses a candidate in the following areas:
          • Verbal Ability: This section assesses English grammar, appropriate usage of the same, and reading comprehension.
          • Reasoning Ability: This section assesses identifying words and numeric patterns, problem-solving, figural and factual analysis, decision-making, propositional reasoning and visual-spatial reasoning.
          • Numerical Ability: This section assesses number system, arithmetic, elementary statistics, data interpretation.
        • Note:
          • The score of this test will be called as NQT Score (Cognitive Skills).
        3. What is Attitudinal Alignment NQT (Psychometric Test)? +
        • Attitude is a broad component covering work styles, personality traits and dispositions relevant to the workplace. Attitudinal Alignment NQT tests the attitude alignment framework relevant for entry-level position and provides an assessment report. The score of this test will be called as Attitudinal Alignment NQT Score.
        • Note:
          • NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is a prerequisite to appear for Attitudinal Alignment NQT (Psychometric Test).
        4. What is Industry NQT? +
        • Industry NQT assesses awareness and understanding on a specific industry in the following areas:
          • Industry structure - domains/sub-domains
          • Operative language, processes and practices
          • Regulatory environment
          • Specific business and technological trends
          • Competitive landscape
        • Each candidate who completes the Industry NQT gets an Industry NQT Score for that particular industry. For example, Industry NQT- Banking. Industry NQTs are planned to be conducted every quarter. The candidates can take the tests again to improve their previous scores, if desired.
        • There are Industry NQTs for multiple industries. The candidates wanting to showcase their expertise in multiple industries can appear for as many Industry NQTs as desired.
          • Note:
            • NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is a prerequisite to appear for Industry NQT.
        5. What is Subject NQT? +
        • Subject NQT assesses knowledge and skill in specific subjects pertaining to job roles in the following areas:
          • Test of Knowledge: Candidates will be assessed on Pseudocode, Algorithms, Programming constructs, basic Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and its stage-wise documents, implementation using C, C++ and Java.
          • Test of Application: Candidates can code in any one of the programming languages like C, C++, Java, Python, and PERL.
          • Hands-on Exercise.
        • Each candidate who completes the Subject NQT gets a Subject NQT Score for that particular subject. For example, Subject NQT - Cloud Computing. Subject NQTs are planned to be conducted every quarter. The candidates can take the tests again to improve their previous scores, if desired.
        • There are Subject NQTs for multiple subjects. The candidates wanting to showcase their expertise in multiple subjects across industries can appear for as many Subject NQTs as desired.
        • Note:
          • NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is a prerequisite to appear for Subject NQTs.
        6. How do I register for NQT? +
          • 1. Click on the "Register Now" button from the banner, then click on "Create Account" button and enter the required details to create your account.
          • 2. Once you are logged in, the TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) registration form opens up.
          • 3. You need to fill in all the required details and click on "Submit" button.
          • 4. Once the details are successfully submitted, you will receive a confirmation through SMS/Email.
          • 5. You can view/edit the application details from the TCS NQT product webpage.
        7. I appeared for NQT in October 2020, I want to register for Subject NQT and Industry NQT. What is the procedure? +
        • 1. As you are already a registered user, click on the "Register Now" button from the banner on TCS NQT webpage.
        • 2. Login using your credentials, the TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) registration form opens up.
        • 3. You will be able to edit your form and fill the required details to register for the February 2021 test, then click on "Submit" button.
        • 4. Once the details are successfully submitted, you will receive a confirmation through SMS/Email.
        8. Who can apply for the TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT)? +
        • Candidates eligible to apply for the TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) should meet the below eligibility criteria:
          • UG, PG and Diploma students in their pre-final and final year of study
          • Students with any degree and specialisation/discipline
          • Freshers with less than 2 years of experience
          • Age limit - minimum 18 years and maximum 28 years
        9. How will the NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) help me in getting a job? +
        • The NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is an assessment of general ability. Many companies conduct their initial selection of candidates based on their general ability scores.
        • In addition, Industry NQT and Subject NQT scores can help you certify your talent in the areas that are relevant to the role that you are aspiring for.
          • TCS is one of the companies that will use NQT Scores for initial selection. TCS iON team is in collaboration with other corporates to acknowledge the NQT Scores, so that you can get a job in any industry or role of your choice.
        • Note:
          • You need to apply for jobs on the respective corporate portal and meet their eligibility criteria.
          • TCS Job Listing portal will be launched shortly and details will be shared. Only candidates with NQT Score will be able to register.
        10. When I took my test in October 2020, Attitudinal Alignment NQT was not available. Will my NQT Score be valid or do I need to reappear for NQT? +
        • You will not have to reappear for the entire NQT, but you need to register only for the Attitudinal Alignment NQT (Psychometric Test). The score will get updated in your TCS NQT Score.
        11. I appeared for NQT in October 2020, can I reappear to secure a better score? +
        • Yes, you can reappear. Only your best score will be mentioned on the score card.
        12. I appeared for NQT, can I take Subject NQT and Industry NQT now? +
        • Yes, NQT Score (Cognitive Skills) is a prerequisite to appear for Subject NQTs and Industry NQTs.
        13. What is the mode of assessment and when will I receive the Hall Ticket? +
        • The mode of assessment is "In-Centre Assessment" and is conducted at TCS iON Authorised Exam Centres.
        • You will receive the Hall Ticket on your email ID, 2 days prior to the assessment. In case you have not received your hall ticket 2 days prior to the assessment, you can reach out to TCS iON HelpDesk (1800 266 6282) for support.
        14. Where can I view my completed registration details? How can I edit my submitted application? +
        • You can view the registration details from 'View Details' on the "TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT)" page, using your email ID mentioned in the TCS NQT registration form. You can edit your application on or before the registration closure date.
        15. I forgot to apply for Subject NQT or Industry NQT. What should I do now? +
        • You can view the registration details from 'View Details' on the "TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT)" page, using your email ID mentioned in the TCS NQT registration form. You can edit your application to include Subject NQT and/or Industry NQT on or before the registration closure date.
        16. Who can apply for Subject NQT or Industry NQT? +
        • Students from any branch or discipline can appear for Subject NQT and Industry NQT to validate their knowledge and skills.
        17. Will both the tests, TCS NQT and Subject NQT, be conducted on the same day? +
        • While the exam may be scheduled either on the same date or on different dates, the dates will not overlap for the various tests that you choose.
        18. Some of the details in my application form are incorrect. Should I apply again? +
        • No, you do not need to apply again. You have to login to the product webpage and click on 'View Details' to view your application. Next you need to click on 'Edit Application' from the right top corner of the screen to edit your application details.
        • In case you face any issues, please reach out to the TCS iON HelpDesk (1800 266 6282). If you have submitted more than one application, then none of the forms will be considered.
        19. How will the results be communicated? +
        • After you complete the test, results will be processed and your scores will be shared to your registered email ID and mobile number that you provided during the test registration process. You can reach out to TCS iON HelpDesk (1800 266 6282).
        20. Is there any cut-off score or percentage to pass the TCS NQT? +
        • There is no cut-off score or PASS/FAIL criteria set to report your performance. You will be given a score that indicates your current ability in the topics that are assessed.
        21. What are the next steps after getting NQT Score? +
        • You can apply to any company that recognises the NQT Score and indicate your TCS NQT registration number and score in the job application form of that company.
        • Depending on the NQT Score you have secured, you can apply to companies and job roles that accept such level of NQT Scores.
        • You can view the list of corporates who have acknowledged the TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) Score on the product webpage.
        • You can directly apply to the corporate for jobs with your NQT Score if you meet their eligibility criteria.
        • You can apply on the TCS iON Job listing portal (details will be shared shortly).
        • Only candidates who have an NQT Score will be eligible for the above mentioned.
        22. Will TCS iON share my score with all the companies to help me get a job? Will TCS iON arrange job interviews for me? +
        • TCS iON will not arrange for any job interviews. You need to apply to the companies yourself. Your NQT Scores will be shared only with those companies that you have applied for a job and when they ask for it.
        • You can view the list of corporates who have acknowledged the TCS National Qualifier Test (TCS NQT) Score on the product webpage.
        • In addition, you can apply on the TCS iON Job Listing portal, as this privilege is given only for the candidates you have appeared for TCS NQT (details will be shared shortly).
        • Note:
          • Each company eligibility/filtering/selection criterion is based on the rules defined by them on the levels of different abilities required for a job and may also have additional eligibility/filtering/selection criteria.
        23. How long will my score be valid? Can I take another attempt to improve my NQT Scores? +
        • Your NQT Score is valid for 2 years from the date of result publishing.
        • There is no limit on the number of attempts. You can write the next TCS NQT (as and when it is announced) to improve your NQT Scores. The highest score from among your attempts, will be considered as your current NQT Score.
